About me

After taking my first progamming course (c++) during my senior year of High School, not having any programming experience before, I was amazed and intrigued on how fun programming can be. After completing the course, there was no doubt in my mind that coding was something that I wanted to do for the rest of my life so thats when I decided to pursue a career as a developer.

Having just graduated with a C.S. degree and finishing up an intensive coding bootcamp, I believe I have the knowledge and skills to join a team of developers to build functional yet beautiful, user friendly web applications!

I'm a true minimalist who believes less is more.
